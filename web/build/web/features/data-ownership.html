Your data is yours

Own your identity, posts, and relationships. Export everything anytime. No lock-in, ever.

What is Data Ownership?

On traditional social platforms, your data belongs to the company. You're locked in—switching platforms means losing your followers, posts, and identity. Kyron changes this. Your data is yours from day one.

Decentralized Identity (DIDs)

Kyron uses Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) built on the AT Protocol. Your identity isn't tied to Kyron—it's portable. You can use the same identity across multiple platforms, or switch platforms entirely while keeping your identity intact.

Export Your Data

With one tap, export your entire Kyron presence:

  • All your posts and media
  • Your follower/following relationships
  • Your profile information
  • Your communities and memberships
  • Your privacy settings and preferences

Built on AT Protocol

Kyron is built on the AT Protocol, the same open standard powering Bluesky. This means:

  • Your data is stored in a personal data repository you control
  • You can run your own server if you want
  • No single company can shut down your access to your data
  • Interoperability with other AT Protocol apps

No Lock-In

We believe the best way to earn your trust is to make it easy to leave. If you decide Kyron isn't for you, take your data and go. Your identity, your posts, your relationships—they're all yours to keep.
