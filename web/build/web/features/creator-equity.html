Own your value

10% of revenue goes to creators. Watch time matters more than follower count.

The Creator Economy Is Broken

On traditional platforms, creators get 0-10% of revenue. Platforms take 70-90%. Algorithms favor big accounts over small creators. Follower count matters more than content quality. It's a rigged game.

Kyron's Creator Equity Model

We believe creators deserve a fair share. That's why 10% of Kyron's revenue goes directly to creators. Not as tips, not as ads, but as a direct revenue share.

How It Works

Every month, we calculate how much time users spent watching each creator's content. That's your watch time. The 10% revenue pool is distributed proportionally to watch time. More watch time = more earnings.

Watch Time, Not Followers

A creator with 100 followers who gets 10,000 hours of watch time earns more than a creator with 100,000 followers who gets 5,000 hours. Quality content that keeps people engaged is rewarded. Follower count is irrelevant.

Monthly Payouts

Earnings are calculated and paid out monthly. No waiting, no mystery algorithms. You can see exactly how much watch time you got and how much you earned. Complete transparency.

Multiple Revenue Streams

The 10% revenue share is just one way to earn. You can also:

  • Sell exclusive content to subscribers
  • Receive tips from fans
  • Monetize AR lenses you create
  • Earn from community memberships
  • Sell digital products

No Ads, No Tracking

Kyron doesn't show ads. We don't track users for ad targeting. Our revenue comes from subscriptions and premium features. This means creators are rewarded for quality content, not for maximizing engagement metrics.
