AR-first creation

Create immersive AR content at 30fps. Built-in lenses, remix tools, and a creator marketplace.

Why AR Matters for Creators

AR is the future of social content. It's more engaging than static posts, more immersive than videos, and more fun to create. But most platforms treat AR as an afterthought. Kyron makes it first-class.

Built-In Lenses at 30fps

Kyron ships with a library of AR lenses you can use immediately. Face filters, object detection, hand tracking, and more. All running at smooth 30fps on mobile devices.

MediaPipe Integration

We use Google's MediaPipe for fast, accurate pose and hand detection. Models are lightweight (under 6MB) and run entirely on-device. No server calls, no latency, no privacy concerns.

Create Your Own Lenses

Use our lens creation toolkit to build custom AR effects. Remix existing lenses, add your own assets, and publish to the marketplace. Earn revenue when others use your lenses.

Remix and Collaborate

Create content with lenses from other creators. Remix, remix, remix. Build on each other's work and create something new. The more you remix, the more visibility you get.

Lens Marketplace

Creators who build popular lenses earn revenue. Every time someone uses your lens, you get a cut. It's a new way to monetize creativity.
