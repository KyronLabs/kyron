Zero-follower discovery

AI-powered feed that surfaces great content regardless of follower count. Your voice matters from day one.

The Problem with Follower-Based Discovery

On traditional platforms, your reach depends on your follower count. A brilliant post from someone with 10 followers gets buried. Meanwhile, mediocre content from accounts with millions of followers floods the feed. This creates a catch-22: you need followers to be seen, but you can't get followers if nobody sees you.

How Kyron's Discovery Works

Kyron uses vector embeddings and AI to rank content by quality, not popularity. Every post is analyzed for relevance, engagement potential, and alignment with your interests. The result: a feed that surfaces great content from creators you've never heard of.

The Technology Behind It

We use Pinecone for vector similarity search and Redis for real-time caching. Posts are embedded using state-of-the-art language models, then ranked based on:

  • Content quality: Grammar, clarity, originality
  • Relevance: How well it matches your interests
  • Engagement potential: Likelihood others will engage
  • Freshness: Recent posts ranked higher
  • Diversity: Avoid showing too much of the same topic

Fair Visibility for All

Whether you have 0 followers or 100,000, your posts get the same fair chance. A brilliant insight from a new creator can go viral. Mediocre content from an influencer gets less visibility. This levels the playing field and rewards quality over popularity.

Transparent Ranking

Unlike algorithmic black boxes, you can see why content is ranked the way it is. We show you the factors that influenced each post's visibility, so you understand how to create content that resonates.
