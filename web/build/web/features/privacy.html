Privacy by design

E2EE on every post. No tracking, no surveillance, no compromises.

Privacy Is a Right, Not a Feature

Most social platforms treat privacy as an afterthought. They track everything: what you click, what you read, who you follow, where you are. Then they sell this data to advertisers. Kyron is different.

End-to-End Encryption

Every post on Kyron can be encrypted end-to-end using the Signal Protocol. Only you and the people you choose can read your posts. Even Kyron can't read them.

Optional, Not Mandatory

You decide which posts are encrypted. Want to share publicly? No encryption. Want to share with just close friends? E2EE. Want to share with a specific group? E2EE to that group. You're in control.

No Tracking

Kyron doesn't track you. We don't use cookies to follow you across the web. We don't know where you are. We don't know what other sites you visit. We don't sell your data. Period.

No Ads, No Surveillance

Kyron doesn't show ads. We don't need to track you to make money. Our revenue comes from subscriptions and premium features. This means we're not incentivized to invade your privacy.

Differential Privacy

When we analyze data to improve Kyron, we use differential privacy techniques. We add noise to the data so individual privacy is protected. We can improve our service without compromising your privacy.

Transparent Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy is written in plain English, not legal jargon. We explain exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. No hidden clauses, no fine print.

Your Data Is Yours

You can export all your data anytime. You can delete your account and all your data. You can move to another platform and take your data with you. Your data is yours, not ours.

Secure Your Posts

Share with confidence on a platform that respects your privacy.
