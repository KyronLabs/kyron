Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements?

You should be passionate about Kyron, active in the community, and willing to promote the platform. No specific background required!

How long does review take?

We typically review applications within 7 days. You'll receive an email with the decision and next steps.

Can I get paid?

Yes! Ambassadors earn revenue share from referrals and can participate in paid promotional campaigns.

What if I'm rejected?

You can reapply after 3 months. We'll provide feedback on how to strengthen your application.
