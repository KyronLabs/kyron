Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ▼

Visit kyron.spidroid.com/app and click "Sign Up". You'll need an email address or phone number. Follow the verification steps and you're ready to go!

Is my data really private? ▼

Yes. Your data is stored in your personal repository that you control. You can optionally enable end-to-end encryption on your posts. We never sell your data.

How do I earn money on Kyron? ▼

Kyron shares 10% of platform revenue with creators based on watch time. The more people watch your content, the more you earn. No follower requirements!

Can I export my data? ▼

Yes. You can export all your data anytime from your account settings. Your data is portable—you can move to another AT Protocol app if you want.

What is AT Protocol? ▼

AT Protocol is an open standard for decentralized social networks. It lets you own your identity and data. Learn more in our AT Protocol guide.

How do I report abuse? ▼

Use the report button on any post or profile. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email support@kyron.spidroid.com.

Our support team is here to help. Reach out anytime.
