Get started in 5 minutes

Create your account, find creators to follow, and make your first post.

1

Create Your Account

Go to kyron.spidroid.com/app and click "Sign Up". Enter your email, create a strong password, and choose your username. Your username is your identity on Kyron—choose something you like.

Pro tip: Your username can be changed anytime, so don't stress if you want to change it later.

2

Set Up Your Profile

Add a profile picture, write a bio, and add a link to your website or portfolio. Your profile is how people discover you, so make it count.

Pro tip: A good bio is 1-2 sentences that explain what you're about. Example: "Designer • Coffee enthusiast • Building cool things"

3

Follow Some Creators

Explore the Discover page and follow creators you're interested in. You don't need to follow many—even 3-5 creators will give you a good feed to start with.

Pro tip: Kyron's AI will recommend creators based on who you follow. The more you interact, the better your recommendations get.

4

Make Your First Post

Click the compose button and write something. It can be a thought, a question, a photo, or a video. Keep it authentic—people connect with genuine content.

Pro tip: Use hashtags to help people discover your post. Kyron's AI will also surface your post to relevant audiences based on content quality.

5

Explore and Engage

Browse your feed, like posts you enjoy, and reply to conversations. Engagement helps Kyron understand your interests and improve your recommendations.

Pro tip: The more you engage, the better your feed becomes. Kyron learns what you like and shows you more of it.

What's Next?

Now that you're set up, here are some things you can explore:

  • • Create AR content: Use our built-in lenses to create immersive posts
  • • Join communities: Find groups around your interests
  • • Enable encryption: Make your posts private with E2EE
  • • Become a creator: Start earning from your content
