Building the future of social

Kyron is a mission-driven team building user-owned social platforms on open protocols. We believe creators and communities deserve better.

Our Mission

We're building a social platform where creators own their data, communities thrive without algorithms controlling them, and everyone has a voice—regardless of follower count.

Traditional platforms extract value from creators and communities. Kyron flips the model: you own your identity, your posts, and your relationships. You decide what you see. You keep the value you create.

Our Values

User Ownership

Your data is yours. You control your identity, posts, and relationships. No lock-in, ever.

Open Protocols

Built on AT Protocol, the same open standard powering Bluesky. Interoperable and decentralized.

Creator Equity

10% of revenue goes to creators. Watch time matters more than follower count.

Privacy First

E2EE on every post. No tracking, no surveillance, no compromises on privacy.

Open Source

MIT licensed. Community-driven development. No gatekeepers.

Zero-Follower Discovery

AI-powered feed surfaces great content regardless of follower count.
